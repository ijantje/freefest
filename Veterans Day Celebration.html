Veterans Day Celebration America’s Freedom Festival is pleased to sponsor the 2014 Veterans Day Celebration on Friday, Nov. 11th at 10 a.m at Timpview High School. Sterling Poulson, the Chief Meteorologist from KUTV, will address the audience. Provo Mayor John Curtis will join in the service. The acclaimed Timpview Band will stir the audience with rousing patriotic numbers. Veterans are invited to attend a luncheon after the event. Please RSVP to Timpview’s main office at 801-221-9720 if you will be attending.
